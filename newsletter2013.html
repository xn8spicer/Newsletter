Broadening and Deepening Health Disparity Research at PRI

Over the past few years, health care has gained increasing national attention in the United States, and policymakers are beginning to make major national changes to increase the quality of and access to health care. However, even with these changes, there is still wide variation among U.S. populations in terms of disease prevalence, health outcomes, and health care access. As such, one of PRI’s major goals is to study health disparities in the United States in the hopes of uncovering the mechanisms underlying such disparities and eventually informing strategies to eliminate inequalities in health outcomes and access to care.
